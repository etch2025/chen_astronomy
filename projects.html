Binary Star Orbit Fitter

Last Updated: 5/24/2026

Takes observation data inputs of angular separation and position angle and plots a fitted orbit using a non-linear least squares method with the primary star at the origin.
Uses Matplotlib, NumPy, and SciPy

Calculates:
- Sky Projected Orbital Elements (a, e, ω)
- Apoapsis and periapsis positions and distances
- Prediction of position angle and angular separation at a given time (WIP)
- Past and future times of apoapsis and periapsis (WIP)
- Residuals

Eclipsing Binary Stars Simulator

Last Updated: 5/25/2026

Simulates a light curve of eclipsing binary stars
Uses Matplotlib and NumPy

Outputs
- Light Curve and Orbital Diagram
- Eclipse Luminosity and Duration
- Points of Contact
- Impact Parameter
- L1 Point (Roche Lobe Overflow)

Assumptions
- Uniform Luminosity Distribution (No limb darkening)
- Circular Orbit (e = 0)
- Celestial bodies m1 and m2 are spherical with circular projected areas

Science Olympiad Exams

Ethan Chen | 2025 SciOly Alumni

Science Olympiad (SciOly) is a team-based competition in which students compete in 23 events across various fields of STEM, from the regional to national level. Students participate in test events, conduct lab experiments, and construct devices. Events are divided into study and build events. Division B and C denotes middle school and high school respectively. Some invitationals/tournaments do not release tests publicly.

I joined the A-Team in the 2025-2026 Season, a group of event supervisors that provides resources for the space science events at the national level.
