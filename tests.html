Science Olympiad (SciOly) is an American team compettion in which students compete in 23 events across various fields of STEM, from the regional to national level. Students participate in test events, conduct chemistry experiments, and even consruct their own rubber-band powered airplane. Events are divided into study and build events. Division B and C denotes middle school and high school respectively.
