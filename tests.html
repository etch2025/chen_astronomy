Science Olympiad Tests

Ethan Chen | 2025 SciOly Alumni

Science Olympiad (SciOly) is a team-based competition in which students compete in 23 events across various fields of STEM, from the regional to national level. Students participate in test events, conduct chemistry experiments, and even construct their own rubber-band powered airplane. Events are divided into study and build events. Division B and C denotes middle school and high school respectively. Some invitationals/tournaments do not release tests publicly.

Being a member of the national A-Team, a group of prominent SciOly Astronomy C and Solar System B/Reach for the Stars B writers coordinating with NASA Chandra and National Science Olympiad, I aim to create and share high-quality Astronomy materials for future competitors and astrophysicists.
