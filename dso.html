Deep Sky Object Image Gallery

The term "Deep Sky Object" (DSO) generally refers to objects outside the Solar System and usually excludes binary/multiple star systems. As such, DSOs refer to star clusters, nebulae, and galaxies.

Some photos were taken at the 2024 Summer Science Program in Astrophysics at CU Boulder, with a 20-inch Planewave Reflector used by Team 5 "Kraft Maggot Cheese." L. Gault, S. Scott, E. Chen
